# Key Slack Statistics in 2024

## FAQ: Slack Statistics 2024

#### What is the total number of daily active users on Slack in 2024?

As of 2024, Slack boasts an estimated 32.3 million daily active users, demonstrating its widespread adoption and engagement across various industries and professional communities.

#### How many organizations use Slack in 2024?

Over 750,000 organizations globally have integrated Slack into their daily operations, leveraging its powerful communication and collaboration tools to enhance productivity and team synergy.

#### What is the revenue generated by Slack in 2023?

Slack generated a remarkable revenue of $5,967 million in 2023, reflecting its significant impact on the market and its role as a key player in the digital communication space.

#### How has Slack's user base grown over the years?

From 2019 to 2023, Slack's monthly active users have seen a consistent uptrend, growing from 14.6 million to 54.1 million. This growth trajectory underscores Slack's increasing popularity and utility as a collaboration platform.

#### What are the most popular categories of apps in the Slack App Directory?

The Slack App Directory is rich with over 2,600 apps, with Productivity being the primary focus, boasting 1,335 apps. Other popular categories include Communication, HR & Team Culture, and Project Management.

#### How many custom apps are used by organizations on Slack?

Beyond the public Slack App Directory, more than 650,000 custom apps are utilized by organizations, tailoring the Slack experience to meet their unique operational needs and enhance their workflow efficiency.

#### How does Slack contribute to Salesforce's revenue?

In the second quarter of 2023, Slack contributed 12% to the total Salesforce revenue, highlighting its substantial contribution to its parent company's financial success post-acquisition in December 2020.

#### What sets Slack apart from its competitors?

Slack distinguishes itself with a market share of 33.95% in the communication category, offering a seamless and integrative platform that supports a wide range of functionalities and third-party apps, fostering a collaborative and efficient work environment.
